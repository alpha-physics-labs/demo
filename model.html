Honest accuracy

Predicted vs. measured — no cherry-picking

Every point is a real material. The diagonal is a perfect prediction. Points sit close to it across the whole range — and where they don't (the hard ceramics like SiC, TiB₂), the model is honest about the gap rather than hiding it.

The headline number — R² 0.84, ±9.3 GPa — is measured on 1,950 materials the model never saw during training, split so no compound leaks between train and test. That is what makes it a real result and not memorization.

The validation model

We bring the model to your data — the data never moves

Ballistic test data can't leave its owner, and it shouldn't have to. ALPHA is built to be validated inside your environment: you keep the data, we bring the model, you see whether it works — before any partnership deepens.

You keep your data

Classified or proprietary test results stay entirely on your side. Nothing is uploaded, shared, or exposed.

We hand you the model

ALPHA runs against your held data and produces predictions with uncertainty on each one — a self-contained tool, not a data request.

You check the score

You compare our predictions to your ground truth and see the accuracy yourself. Trust is earned on your terms, with your data.

✓ What this is

A reproducible screening tool that predicts material properties from chemistry, with honest uncertainty and a stated evidence level — built to be validated inside a partner's environment.

✕ What this is not

Not a claim of ballistic stopping power, and not armor validation. It predicts a material property, not V50. Ballistic performance is a system property that needs real test data to learn.
